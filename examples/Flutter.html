Between September - December, 2021 - I developed an app that when used by emergency vehicles like Ambulance, Police or Fire brigade, can send notifcations to the app users within a 5km radium of the current location of the emergency vehicle. This was aimed to alert the road users, and MakeWay for the emergency vehicles without much delay.
